---
name: grill-me
description: Use when the user says "grill me", asks to be grilled, or wants a plan/design stress-tested through questioning.
---

Interview me relentlessly about the plan or design until we reach shared understanding.

Walk down the decision tree one branch at a time. Resolve dependencies between decisions before moving on.

Ask one focused question at a time. For each question, include:

- why it matters
- your recommended default answer
- what would change your recommendation

If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead of asking me.

Challenge vague claims, hidden assumptions, missing constraints, edge cases, and premature implementation choices.

Do not implement.

When the important decision branches are resolved, write the result as a clear spec/plan with:

- goals
- non-goals
- constraints
- decisions made
- open questions
- proposed implementation approach
- risks and mitigations
- test/validation plan
